Parks & Recreation Infographic

I created this infographic about one of my favorite TV shows, Parks and Recreation. Since it is a comedy show, I wanted to convey a lighthearted, fun tone and chose mostly pastel colors. I decided to keep the characters faceless to emphasize their already unique features, and to maintain its simplicity.

San Francisco Poster

I created a poster for my favorite city using Adobe Illustrator. I aimed to create a background with a repeating pattern that represented San Francisco. I chose to draw the Painted Ladies because of the houses' array of unique colors, and they helped reinforce a personal "home" aspect of the city.

Comic-Inspired Piece

I created this fun comic-book style illustration and wanted to put some emotion into the fictional character. I think the purple hair and corresponding eyeshadow of the woman create a more dramatic effect, and having a yellow background juxtaposes her sadness.

Totoro Recreated

I replicated this scene from Totoro to play around with the gradient and blend features of Adobe Illustrator.

Personal Logo

I wanted to create a minimalist logo, and I approached this by keeping my initials lowercase. The scribbled pen line across my initials represents my constant desire to doodle. The transparent, pastel green background provides a little color so that the design is still simple and minimalist without being too plain at the same time.
